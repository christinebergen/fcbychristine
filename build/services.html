Foot Care Services offered by Christine

Foot Care by Christine boasts extensive experience and extra education in foot care nursing, specializing in:

  • Nursing Foot Care treatment
  • Reduction of corns and calluses
  • Thick, fungal nails
  • Full foot assessment
  • Moisturizer applied (if desired)
  • Referrals to other health care diciplines if necessary
  • Trimming & Smoothing of long and thick toe nails
  • Discussion of foot health focusing on maintaining independence and mobility

Non-surgical Nail corrections now available!

The Onyfix nail correction system is an innovative product (medical device) that is an effective and painless treatment of almost all forms of involuted and ingrown toenails. Click here to visit Onyfix's website or inquire in person.
