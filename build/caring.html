Caring Corner

Clothing Donations:

Foot Care by Christine accepts donations of gently used shoes, boots, jackets, scarves, gloves, and toques, which are then donated to unhoused individuals, low-income seniors, and persons in need.

Wellness Equipment Network:

Foot Care by Christine will help facilitate donations of used medical equipment within our client base.


If you have something to offer, please let us know at your next appointment and we will try to find it a good home. Alternatively, if you're in need of mobility equipment, let us know and we might be able to locate it for you.


Equipment must be offered free, and be in good useable condition.

Puzzle Exchange

Do you have any jigsaw puzzles at home that you have already done and don't want to do again? Are you looking for some new jigsaw puzzles to do? We offer a puzzle donation/exchange.


If you're interested in either getting a new puzzle, or giving someone else a new-to-them puzzle, please ask about it at your next appointment!
