Foot Care Education by Christine

Foot Care Education Sessions for Seniors

Educational sessions for Seniors are offered on the following topics:

  • Answering questions about when Foot Care would be recommended to be done by a Foot Care Nurse
  • How to pick a Foot care Nurse & questions to ask
  • Tips and Tricks on how to continue Foot Care independently
  • Education for Caregivers on the same topics

All Education sessions are designed to be approximately 1 - 1.5 hours long, however, in the past if the participating group is large, it can go over that time.

Please contact us for more information!
